# WrightTest Trademark and Branding Policy

The name "WrightTest", the WrightTest logo, and related branding are owned by
the WrightTest project owner.

You may use the WrightTest name only to truthfully refer to the original
WrightTest project, for example:

- "built with WrightTest"
- "compatible with WrightTest"
- "forked from WrightTest"
- "based on WrightTest"

You may not use the WrightTest name, logo, or branding in a way that suggests
your fork, product, hosted service, or commercial offering is the official
WrightTest project.

You may not use the WrightTest name or branding for a competing hosted service
or standalone commercial product without written permission.
